## **What Is Offshore Software Development?**

Offshore software development refers to the practice of hiring a software team located in another country to design, build, test, or maintain digital products. Rather than relying solely on in-house staff, businesses partner with an offshore software development company to reduce costs, access specialized talent, and improve delivery speed.

This model is widely adopted across three main engagement types: project-based outsourcing for defined scope and timelines, dedicated development teams that work as a long-term product extension, and staff augmentation to fill specific technical gaps within an existing team.


## **Why Businesses Choose Offshore Software Development Companies in Vietnam**

Vietnam has built a strong reputation in the global software outsourcing market over the past two decades. Several factors make it a practical and attractive destination.

**Competitive development costs.** Compared to Western markets like the US, UK, or Australia, development rates in Vietnam are significantly lower, allowing businesses to optimize budgets without compromising on technical quality.

**Large and skilled talent pool.** Vietnam produces a growing number of software engineers skilled in modern technologies such as Java, .NET, React, Node.js, Python, Flutter, AWS, and Azure.

**Proven outsourcing experience.** Many Vietnamese companies have years of experience collaborating with international clients from North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, making them familiar with Agile workflows, global delivery standards, and remote collaboration tools.

**Fast team scaling.** Vietnamese offshore vendors can typically onboard talent faster than local markets in many Western countries, giving businesses greater speed and flexibility when launching new products or expanding capacity.
