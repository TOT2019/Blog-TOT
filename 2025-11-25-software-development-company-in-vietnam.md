## Types of Software Outsourcing Models

When working with a software development company in Vietnam, businesses can choose from several engagement models:

- **Onshore Outsourcing**: Contracting a service provider located in the same country, offering easier communication and time zone alignment.
- **Offshore Outsourcing**: Partnering with companies in different countries like Vietnam, providing cost advantages and access to diverse talent pools.
- **Nearshore Outsourcing**: Working with providers in neighboring countries for a balance of cost savings and proximity.
